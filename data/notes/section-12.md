## Section XII Notes

1. This Section does not cover:
  (a) artificial fur or articles thereof (heading 4304 or 6703, as the case may be), except footwear or headgear or parts thereof;
  (b) worn clothing or other worn articles of heading 6309;
  (c) orthopaedic appliances, including crutches, surgical belts and trusses (heading 9021).

2. (A) Goods classifiable in Chapters 64 or 65 which are parts of such goods are not classified in those Chapters and are classified in Chapter 62 where they are of materials described in Section XI.
  (B) Goods classifiable in Chapter 66 are classified in that Chapter even if made wholly or partly of textile materials.

3. For the purposes of Chapters 64 to 67:
  (a) articles of textile materials and footwear or headgear of any material, incompletely made up, whether or not assembled, but clearly identifiable as intended for one of the products falling within those Chapters, are to be classified in those Chapters and not elsewhere;
  (b) such articles, when presented unassembled or disassembled, are to be classified in the same heading as the assembled article.
