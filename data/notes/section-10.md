## Section X Notes

1. This Section does not cover:
  (a) photographic transparencies of heading 4911;
  (b) papers, cements and other products of heading 4814 (for example, lacquered papers, metallised papers);
  (c) toilet paper and similar articles of heading 4818;
  (d) stamping foils of heading 3212;
  (e) sensitised paper or paperboard of headings 3701 to 3704.
