## Chapter 96 Notes

1. This Chapter does not cover:
  (a) pencils for cosmetic or toilet uses (Chapter 33);
  (b) articles of Chapter 66 (for example, parts of umbrellas or walking-sticks);
  (c) imitation jewellery (heading 71.17);
  (d) parts of general use, as defined in Note 2 to Section XV, of base metal (Section XV), or similar goods of plastics (Chapter 39);
  (e) cutlery or other articles of Chapter 82 with handles or other parts of carving or moulding materials. When presented separately, such handles and other parts are to be classified in heading 96.01 or 96.02;
  (f) articles of Chapter 90 (for example, spectacle frames (heading 90.03), mathematical drawing pens (heading 90.17), brushes of a kind specialised for use in dentistry or for medical, surgical or veterinary purposes (heading 90.18));
  (g) articles of Chapter 91 (for example, clock or watch cases);
  (h) musical instruments or parts or accessories thereof (Chapter 92);
  (ij) articles of Chapter 93 (arms and parts thereof);
  (k) articles of Chapter 94 (for example, furniture, luminaires and lighting fittings);
  (l) articles of Chapter 95 (toys, games, sports requisites); or
  (m) works of art, collectors' pieces or antiques (Chapter 97).

2. In Note 1 above, the reference to "parts" does not include references to sheets or plates or other parts of carving or moulding materials. For the purposes of headings 96.01 and 96.02, "carving or moulding materials" means:
  (a) hard animal materials (for example, ivory, bone, tortoise-shell, horn, antlers, coral, mother-of-pearl) and articles of these materials;
  (b) jet and minerals substituting for jet;
  (c) amber and agglomerated amber, meerschaum and agglomerated meerschaum; and
  (d) hard vegetable materials (for example, corozo) and articles of these materials.

3. For the purposes of heading 96.06, the expression "slide fasteners" means articles consisting of two rows of teeth of base metal, plastics or other material, each row fixed on a tape, and a slide, by the operation of which the two rows of teeth can be interlocked or separated.

4. Heading 96.06 does not cover press-fasteners (heading 96.06 does not apply), having no chain scoops or flanges on the tape, but consisting of interlocking parts affixed to the tape in a series. Such goods are classified as press-fasteners of heading 96.07.

5. For the purposes of heading 96.14, the expression "smoking pipes and pipe bowls" does not include pipes or pipe bowls made of precious metal or of metal clad with precious metal.
