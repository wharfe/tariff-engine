## Section IX Notes

1. This Section does not cover:
  (a) cork in the form of stoppers, discs, balls and similar shapes, including blanks for stoppers, of heading 45.03;
  (b) articles of stone or of other mineral substances (Chapter 68); or
  (c) worked ivory, bone, tortoise-shell, horn, antlers, coral, mother-of-pearl and other animal carving material, or articles of these materials (heading 96.01).

2. References in this Section to "wood" apply also to bamboos and other materials of a woody nature.

3. References in this Section to plaiting materials or to basketware include strips, osier, reeds, rushes, rattans, bamboos, raffia, straw, cereal bark and similar natural plaiting materials.
