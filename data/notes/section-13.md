## Section XIII Notes

1. This Section does not cover:
  (a) articles of Chapter 25 (for example, cements);
  (b) coated or polished paper and paperboard of heading 4810 or 4811 (for example, those coated with mica powder or graphite, bituminised or asphalted paper and paperboard);
  (c) coated, impregnated or covered textile fabric of Chapter 56 or 59 (for example, fabric coated or covered with ceramic powder, bituminised or asphalted fabric);
  (d) articles of Chapter 71;
  (e) tools or parts of tools, of Chapter 82;
  (f) goods of heading 8306;
  (g) machines or appliances (for example, pumps) or parts thereof (for example, metal gaskets), of Section XVI, including refractory constructional goods of heading 6909 when they form parts of such machines or appliances;
  (h) ball or roller bearings of heading 8482;
  (ij) articles of Chapter 85 (for example, electrical insulators (heading 8546));
  (k) artificial teeth (heading 9021);
  (l) articles of Chapter 91 (for example, clocks or clock cases);
  (m) articles of Chapter 95 (for example, toys, games or sports requisites);
  (n) articles of heading 9606 (for example, buttons) or of heading 9614 (for example, smoking pipes);
  (o) articles of Chapter 97 (for example, works of art).
